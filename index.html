About Smart Pot:

Smart Pot, the self-watering pot that takes care of your plants for you. Our innovative technology measures the moisture level of the soil and automatically waters your plants as needed, ensuring that they always receive the right amount of water to stay healthy and thrive.

Our Mission:

We believe that plant care should be easy and accessible to everyone. Our mission is to create products that simplify plant care and promote a healthy lifestyle. We are committed to using innovative technology to help people enjoy the benefits of gardening without the hassle.

~ ~ ~ Introduction ~ ~ ~

  • This idea of this project was to make a self sustainable plant pot, which would make owning a plant and maintaining it much easier and would encourage more people to grow their own plants at home
  • People have always wanted to grow plants at their homes but to their disappointment have generally found out that their plant wilts in a few weeks.
  • The aim of this project was to solve this common problem in the most efficient and cheap way possible.

~ ~ ~ Methodology ~ ~ ~

  • Soil moisture sensors are used to detect the moisture level in the soil
  • If the moisture in the soil is below the amount the given plant needs, then the machine turns on the pump to water the pot us9ing drip irrigation.
  • Once the soil reaches the desired moisture level, the computer turns off the pump.
  • This automation along with the use of drip irrigation ensures that water is used in the most efficient way and there is practically no wastage of water.
  • The only thing the user has to do is to refill the water container; when the water in the reservoir is close to getting over, the pot will beep a buzzer to indicate that the user needs to refill the water in the container.

~ ~ ~ Results & Findings ~ ~ ~

  • This method of watering plants is really cost efficient and water efficient as well as it results in almost no wastage of water.
  • The user of the machine has to do no work in taking care of the plant other than refilling the container every few days.
  • The user does not have to worry about their plants dying anymore.

~ ~ ~ Components Used ~ ~ ~

Arduino Uno

It is a relatively inexpnsive micro controller board which acts as the brain of our project. It is the component which decides when to switch on the water pump based off the data sent to it by the Soil Moisture Sensor

Moisture Sensor

It is the most important sensor of this project. It detects the amount of moisture in the soil. We have added 2 of these to increase accuracy. Depending on the extent of water in the soil it outputs an integer between 0 - 1024.

Water Level Sensor

It is used to detect the level of water remaining in the container. It outputs an integer between 0 - 1024 based on the level of water remaining in the container. When water drops below a certain level, it triggers the buzzer

Water Pump

It is the device used to transfer water from the container to the pot. It is switched on only when soil moisture sensor classify the soil as dry. It can pump water using great force

Buzzer

It emits a sound of a particular frequency when a voltage of 5 volts is applied on its terminals. It is triggered when the water level sensor detects the water level in the container to is low.
